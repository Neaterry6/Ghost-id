Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about the sanctuary.

Is my data really deleted?
expand_more
Yes. When you delete a message or a thread, it is wiped using military-grade zeroing on your local storage and immediately purged from our relay caches.
How do global communities work?
expand_more
Global communities are interest-based clusters. You join with a temporary alias that resets every 24 hours to ensure your long-term identity remains hidden.
Can I recover my account?
expand_more
Because we store zero personal data (no email, no phone number), account recovery is only possible via your local 24-word recovery phrase. Guard it well!
